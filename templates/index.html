Get a key and use it in your HTTP calls



- or -

Get your key and use it anywhere in your HTTP call:


In your path: 

http:///testmycall/newdata/<key>

In your HTTP headers: 

X-Debug-My-HTTP: <key>

Put it in the HTTP body with: 

$ curl -X POST -d '<key>' http:///testmycall/
